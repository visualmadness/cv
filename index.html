Hi! My name is Kevin and I live in Peabody, Massachusetts with my girlfriend and our 4 pets. I've been doing cardistry and magic for over 20 years now, and I have a brother and a sister who both live in the same state as me. I used to be a part of this group called Virtuoso, but I'm no longer a member. From 2020-2022, I ran a company called 'Momentum' that released simple symbolic designs on playing cards, and it did moderately well and earned me some pocket money on the side. Now, I've shifted my focus to learning how to code, and I'm excited to start on this new journey with you all!
